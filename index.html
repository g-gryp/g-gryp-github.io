Projects & Techincal Ideas ABOUT CONTACT

Hi, I'm George.

I'm a biomedical engineeer and I like electronics and neuroscience. This is a collection of ideas, projects and random thoughts.

Notes - AC coupling Instrumentation Amplifiers

Personal notes/thoughts on the different ways of removing DC offset in differential recordings that arose during the writing of my undergraduate thesis on artefact suppression for recording during Deep Brain Stimulation.

Project - Decoding Movement from Neural Signals

Masters level group project for the "Brain Machine Interfaces" module at Imperial College London. Task was to design a MATLAB-based neural decoder to drive a hypothetical prosthetic device.
Coursework description.

Notes - Evaluation of Simple Estimators

Notes that expand on the concepts convered in the first part of the "Advanced Signal Processing" module at Imperial College London. Includes some discussion on the optimal number of bins for histogram estimation of a normal distribution.

Project - Design and Analysis of Instrument for Measurement of Bioelectric Signals

Individual summer project in the Drakakis lab. Design and PCB layout of a 3-channel device, including a transimpedance channel for pA range currents. Thorough analysis of non-idealities and the effects of interfacing with GΩ range sensors.
